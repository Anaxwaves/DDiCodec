Nakamichi vs. DDi Code for Dolby B/C

  
For those who is digitizing cassette audio into computer files, or recording digital audio to analog cassette tapes, DDi Code introduces a brand new option to precisely encode/decode Dolby-B/C in digital domain! Listen to the following audio demos in which the software is compared with a legendary Nakamichi CR-7A cassette deck in encoding/decoding Dolby-B and C:

Demo #1 (Symphny)
Demo #2 (Electronica)
Demo #3 (Vocal)
